Tips for Working Mothers

Try out these tips for working moms.

  • Teach cleanup. Do you clean up toys, hang up coats, stow shoes, pick up laundry, and make beds?
  • Delegate your daily chores. Ask your kids/spouse to help you delegate
  • Plan your morning and plan a work schedule.
  • Plan meals ahead.Also schedule quality family time.
  • Last but not the least make time for yourself.
